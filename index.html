landscape photography

Landscape photography is a massive area of photography art. It is usually an outdoor activity because it is generally focusing on the natural environment more. According to Photography Life, "Landscape photography is the art of capturing pictures of nature and the outdoors in a way that brings your viewer into the scene.” (Photography Life, n.d.) This shows that for the definition of landscape photography, this is like a way that people are recording the outdoor world by taking photos. Landscape photography could also be divided into different parts—for example, Astrophotography, Normal landscape photography, Microphotography, construction photography and seascape photography.

Portrait photography

Portrait photography is a kind of flexible category in photography art, which means it could be either outdoor or indoor activity; the only difference between outdoor and indoor activities is that. Doing portrait photography at the outdoors can quickly get endless exciting backgrounds, but difficult to control the lights. However, to do portrait photography in the studio can adjust the shine very quickly. This is a kind of photography that focuses on people, not the outdoor environment. According to Nate Torres ” Portrait photography is about capturing the essence, personality, identity and attitude of a person utilizing backgrounds, lighting and posing.” (Torres N, n.d.) It shows that portrait photography is a kind of art that was looking for background and making light environment for people, not a natural environment.
